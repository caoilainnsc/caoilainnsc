About Me

I am a rising sophomore at Pomona College in Claremont, California. I am originally from Blacksburg, Virginia where I lived for my entire life up until I started college. I am majoring in Cognitive Science and Computer Science with a specific interest in computaitonal linguistics. While in high school, I worked with Dr. John Brummette at Radford University's SMART (Social Media Analytics and Research Technology) Lab on a comparative content and engagement analysis of nutrition advice on social media between professionals and non-professionals. I am currently working with Dr. Galia Bar-Sever at Pomona College on research looking into the evolution of English slang words over time on Twitter. In addition, I am spending this summer at Old Dominion University working on a paid Research Experience for Undergraduates internship funded by the National Science foundation on Disinformation Detection and Analytics. Specifically, I am working with Dr. Anne Perrotti on analyzing teaching strategies and programs for youth in dentetion and jail.
